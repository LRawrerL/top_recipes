Spaghetti and Meatballs

Description

With a few time-saving tricks, spoon-tender meatballs can be yours in about an hour.

Ingredients

  • ¼ cup extra-virgin olive oil, plus more
  • 5 garlic cloves, sliced
  • 4 tsp salt
  • 50oz of tomato passata
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • half cup seasoned Italian breadcrumbs
  • 1 large egg
  • half onion
  • One pound ground beef

Preparation

  1. Heat ¼ cup extra-virgin olive oil in a medium Dutch oven or heavy pot over medium. Add 5 garlic cloves, sliced; season with kosher salt. Cook, stirring, until fragrant (dont let garlic brown), about 30 seconds. Add two 24.5-oz. bottles tomato passata. Add ½ cup water to one of the bottles, swirl around, and pour into pot. Give sauce a good stir and bring to a vigorous simmer. Reduce heat to low, partially cover, and simmer gently until sauce is slightly thickened, 15 to 20 minutes. Cover and keep warm.
