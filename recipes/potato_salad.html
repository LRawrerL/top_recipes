Korean Potato Salad

Description

Often served in a tidy scoop, this creamy, tangy, subtly sweet potato salad goes great with whatevers coming off the grill.

Ingredients

  • 2 Potato
  • 1 Hard Boil Egg
  • Half a cucumber
  • 2 Table spoon of mayonnaise

Preparation

  1. Boil the potatoes and cut them into 1.5 inch pieces
  2. Chop the hard boil egg and cucumbers
  3. Mash the potatoes and add the chopped hard boil eggs and cucumbers into the potatoes.
  4. Incorporate mayonnaise
